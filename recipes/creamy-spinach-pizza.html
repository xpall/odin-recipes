Creamy Spinach Pizza


Description

Creamy Spinach Pizza is made with creamy ricotta, spinach, Italian herbs, and a mixture of mozzarella
and Parmesan cheeses to capture the classic Florentine flavor in pizza form.

Ingredients

  • 4 bunches spinach pick the leaves rinse and drain well
  • 2 tablespoons Butter
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 piece onion chopped
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1 packet All Purpose Cream
  • 2 teaspoons Seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons grated cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Mayonnaise
  • 2 pieces large store-bought pizza dough
  • 2 pieces tomatoes sliced
  • 1 1/2 cup grated mozzarella
  • 4 tablespoons Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot, season with salt and blanch spinach leaves for 10 seconds. Strain and immediately drop in ice-cold water.
    Drain and squeeze excess water from spinach leaves. Coarsely chop and set aside. (10mins)
  2. Saut√© garlic and onion in butter. Pour water and All Purpose Cream. Season with seasoning, pepper and nutmeg.
  3. Stir in blanched spinach and cook for 1 minute. Turn off heat and stir in grated cheese and mayonnaise. Set aside. (10mins)
  4. Spread half of creamed spinach on pizza dough, top with tomato, mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
    Bake in a preheated oven for 10 minutes or until golden brown. (15mins)
  5. Top with the rest of the creamed spinach. Cut into eight serving pieces and serve. (5mins)
