Filipino Pork Paksiw


Description

Filipino pork paksiw is a deliciously tangy and savory dish, featuring tender pork simmered in a flavorful blend of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices. The addition of banana blossoms adds a unique touch,
creating a delightful culinary experience that perfectly complements steamed rice. This traditional Filipino favorite captures the essence of home cooking, offering a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Pork (pork belly, pork shoulder, or pork hock) - about 1 to 1.5 pounds, cut into chunks
  • Vinegar - 1/2 to 3/4 cup
  • Soy sauce - 1/4 cup
  • Water - 1/2 to 1 cup
  • Garlic - 4 to 6 cloves, minced
  • Peppercorns - 1 teaspoon
  • Bay leaves - 2 to 3 pieces
  • Brown sugar - 1 to 2 tablespoons
  • Banana blossoms (dried or fresh) - 1 cup (if using dried, rehydrate before cooking)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Cooking oil - 1 tablespoon (optional, for sautéing the garlic)

    • These quantities are approximate and can be adjusted based on personal preferences and taste.

Steps

  1. In a large pot or deep pan, heat the cooking oil (if using) over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the pork chunks to the pot and brown them on all sides, stirring occasionally for about 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the vinegar, soy sauce, and water. Add the peppercorns and bay leaves. Stir well to combine.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then lower the heat to a simmer. Cover the pot and let the pork cook for about 1 to 1.5 hours or until the meat becomes tender.
  5. Once the pork is tender, add the brown sugar and salt to taste. Stir until the sugar has dissolved.
  6. Add the banana blossoms to the pot and continue to simmer for an additional 10 to 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  7. Taste the paksiw and adjust the seasoning if needed, adding more salt or sugar to achieve your desired balance of flavors.
  8. Serve the delicious Filipino pork paksiw hot with steamed rice. Enjoy this tangy and savory dish with your family and friends!
